# Category 09: Reflection (振り返り) - Deep Dive Prompt v1.0.0

## Purpose

Extract **synthesized insights** about patterns, progress, and what's working.

---

## Key Questions

### Highlights & Progress
- **What went well recently?**
- **Wins and achievements this period?**
- **What are you proud of?**
- **Progress toward goals?**

**Listen for:**
- "Best part of this month was..."
- "I accomplished..."
- "I'm proud that..."
- "Progress includes..."

### Patterns & Themes
- **What patterns do you notice?**
- **What themes keep coming up?**
- **What's working well? What's not?**
- **Repeated successes or failures?**

**Listen for:**
- "I've noticed..."
- "The pattern is..."
- "Common theme is..."
- "Works well when..."

### Emotional Arc
- **How's your energy and motivation?**
- **Stress levels?**
- **Overall mood/satisfaction?**
- **Changes in emotional state?**

**Listen for:**
- "I'm feeling..."
- "Energy is..."
- "Motivation for..."
- "Stressed about..."

### Next Actions
- **What's next?**
- **Priorities for coming period?**
- **What needs attention?**

**Listen for:**
- "Next I'll..."
- "Priority is..."
- "Need to focus on..."

```json
{

  "suggested_format": "## [Period]\n**Highlights:** ...\n**Patterns:** ...\n**Energy/Motivation:** ...\n**Next Actions:** ...",

}
```
