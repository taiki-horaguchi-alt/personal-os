# Category 05: Constraints (制約条件) - Deep Dive Prompt v1.0.0

## Purpose

Extract **your limitations and boundaries**: what you can't change vs. what you can optimize.

---

## Key Questions

### Unchangeable Constraints
- **What constraints are you stuck with?** (Physical, legal, time-based)
- **Family or location constraints?**
- **Skill gaps that would take years to fix?**
- **Financial limitations?**
- **What can't you change about your situation?**

**Listen for:**
- "I can't..."
- "I'm limited by..."
- "I have to..."
- "I'm stuck with..."

### Changeable Constraints
- **What could you change if you prioritized it?**
- **What's holding you back that's actually under your control?**
- **What would require sacrifice but is possible?**

**Listen for:**
- "I could... if I..."
- "I'm choosing not to because..."
- "I could change this by..."
