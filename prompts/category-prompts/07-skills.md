# Category 07: Skills & Assets (スキル・資産) - Deep Dive Prompt v1.0.0

## Purpose

Extract **your strengths, competencies, and valuable relationships**.

---

## Key Questions

### Hard Skills (テクニカルスキル)
- **What technical or professional skills do you have?**
- **Tools, languages, frameworks you're proficient in?**
- **Certifications or credentials?**
- **What could you teach others?**

**Listen for:**
- "I'm proficient in..."
- "I know how to..."
- "I'm an expert in..."

### Soft Skills
- **What people skills do you bring?**
- **Leadership, communication, negotiation abilities?**
- **How do others describe your strengths?**

**Listen for:**
- "People say I'm good at..."
- "I'm skilled at..."
- "I bring..."

### Meta-Skills
- **How good are you at learning new things?**
- **Systems thinking? Big picture understanding?**
- **Adaptability to new situations?**

**Listen for:**
- "I learn quickly..."
- "I'm good at figuring things out..."

### Network & Relationships (人脈)
- **Who are the important people in your network?**
- **What can they help you with?**
- **Who would you call for advice/help?**

**Listen for:**
- "I know someone who..."
- "My network includes..."
- "Important relationships..."
